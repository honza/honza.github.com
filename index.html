About

I have a great interest in functional programming languages like Clojure and Haskell. I have recently started learning iOS and Android development more intensively. I love all things open source. You can also find me on Twitter and Github.

I work full-time at StrataLabs. I'm the CTO of Radon Metrics and CrowdCE.
