Why Nokia chose Windows over Android

Today, we have all heard about the new deal struck between Nokia and Microsoft. It is a major change in Nokia’s strategy which will without a doubt affect the smartphone market.

A few month ago, the Executive Vice President of Nokia announced that switching to Android is like peeing in your boots for warmth. Well, let me say this: if switching to Android is like peeing in your boots, switching to Windows is like shitting your pants.

Perhaps, Nokia liked the latter better.
