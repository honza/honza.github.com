10 chapters

  • You get through the less glamorous parts while still being nourished.
  • It’s not a read-your-bible-in-a-year plan. It’s more of a plan for reading the Bible as a way of life. You’re never done.
  • You don’t read the Bible so many times in a year. You read Acts 12 times, all gospels 4 times, etc.
  • You always read different things together.
  • There isn’t as much guilt when skipping a day because you don’t fall behind.
  • 10 chapters a day might be a lot for some people but I find it a nice balance.
